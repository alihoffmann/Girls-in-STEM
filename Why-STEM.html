Why STEM?

STEM: (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math)

STEM has gained popularity in the recent years. STEM provides many opportunities for students to explore new options. It encompasses a wide variety of fields which makes it flexible. Although many students can be afraid or discouraged from becoming interested in STEM, it encourages students to push their boundaries.
