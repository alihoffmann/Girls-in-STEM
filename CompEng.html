What is Computer Engineering?

computer engineering (n): the discipline of engineering that embodies the science and technology of design, construction, implementation, and maintenance of software and hardware components of modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment. src: Chula Engineering


But what does this mean?




Computer hardware engineers research, design, develop, and test computer systems and components such as processors, circuit boards, memory devices, networks, and routers.

Let's break that down a bit further...

Computer engineers work to design most parts of a computer. They do everything from researching better types of hard drives to figuring out how to make computers more efficient. Please remember that computer engineers can be very similar to computer scientists. Computer engineers typically work to design computer hardware and develop computer software.
