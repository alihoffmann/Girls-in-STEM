Sarah Adler

Who is she?

She is the CTO (Chief Technology Officer) of Spoon University, "a global community of young influencers shaping the future of food."


Although she never majored in computer science, she taught herself how to code and built Secret Sauce with knowledge she gained from experience!

What does she do?

She created Secret Sauce, a web-based program to introduce students to the Spoon University community and enables students to publish their content.
